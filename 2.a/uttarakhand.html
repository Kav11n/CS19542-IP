Special Features of Uttarakhand

Natural Beauty

  • Home to the majestic Himalayas with peaks like Nanda Devi and Trishul.
  • Picturesque hill stations like Nainital, Mussoorie, and Almora.
  • The Valley of Flowers, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its diverse flora.

Spiritual Significance

  • Known as the 'Land of the Gods' with sacred temples like Kedarnath and Badrinath.
  • The holy river Ganga originates from Gaumukh in Uttarakhand.
  • Haridwar and Rishikesh, renowned for their spiritual significance and yoga centers.

Adventure and Wildlife

  • Popular for adventure sports like trekking, river rafting, and skiing.
  • Jim Corbett National Park, India's oldest national park known for its tigers.
  • Rajaji National Park, rich in biodiversity with elephants, leopards, and more.

Cultural Heritage

  • Traditional folk dances like Garhwali and Kumaoni.
  • Rich cuisine with dishes like Aloo Ke Gutke, Kumaoni Raita, and Bal Mithai.
  • Handicrafts like woolen shawls, ringal crafts, and wood carving.
