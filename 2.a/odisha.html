Special Features of Odisha

Culture and Heritage

  1. Home to the famous Jagannath Temple in Puri, a major pilgrimage site.
  2. Konark Sun Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  3. Rich tradition of classical dance, Odissi, one of the eight classical dance forms of India.

Natural Beauty

  1. Beautiful beaches like Puri Beach and Gopalpur-on-Sea.
  2. Chilika Lake, the largest coastal lagoon in India and the second largest in the world.
  3. Scenic hills and forests in regions like Simlipal and Daringbadi.

Festivals and Traditions

  1. Rath Yatra, a grand chariot festival attracting millions of devotees.
  2. Durga Puja and Diwali celebrated with great fervor.
  3. Chhau Dance, a traditional tribal martial dance form.

Arts and Crafts

  1. Intricate silver filigree work known as Tarakasi.
  2. Pattachitra, traditional scroll painting depicting mythological narratives.
  3. Applique work of Pipili, famous for its vibrant colors and designs.
